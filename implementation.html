Our Four-Step Approach:

Data Maturity Assessment

The first step in our approach is conducting a thorough data maturity assessment of your organization. This involves a deep analysis of your current data infrastructure, processes, and data-related capabilities. Our professionals collaborate with your team to gain a solid understanding of your goals, objectives, and existing challenges.

Our journey begins by gaining a profound understanding of your data landscape. Through thorough assessment, we gauge your data context and maturity level. This step lays the foundation for a strategy that isn't just relevant, but uniquely tailored to your organization's needs.

Back to top

Use Case Validation

Once we have a comprehensive understanding of your data maturity, we move on to help you validate use cases that align with your business objectives. We work closely with your team to identify potential use cases that can deliver tangible value to your organization.

Validation of use cases is pivotal. Together with you, we calculate the potential Return on Investment (ROI), outlining the resource requirements needed for a successful implementation. This is more than just planning; it's sculpting a roadmap that ensures your journey is informed, strategic, and poised for success.

Back to top

Proof of Concept

With validated use cases in hand, we enter the proof of concept (PoC) phase. This involves agile development of small-scale prototypes or experiments to demonstrate the feasibility and potential impact of the proposed solutions.

Architecting solutions is where concepts take tangible shape. Guided by service design principles and an agile methodology, Estedic engineers solutions that seamlessly blend with your environment. The proof of concept phase transforms ideas into actionable realities, laying the groundwork for transformative results.

Back to top

Project Management

When the PoC is successful, we guide you to full-scale implementation with our data and AI project management expertise. Estedic ensures a smooth execution of the solutions, closely collaborating with your team to achieve the desired outcomes.

A solution's value is measured by its sustained impact. Estedic ensures that the solutions we create are scalable as your business evolves. We offer continuous support, stabilize operations, and ensure compliance with relevant regulations. Our partnership extends far beyond implementation; it's a commitment to your ongoing success.
