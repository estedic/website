Empowering Organizations with Data And AI Solutions

Our mission is to empower organizations to realize significant returns on their data investments. As an Amsterdam-based data and AI consultancy company founded in 2021, we have swiftly earned a reputation for delivering top-tier solutions to clients both locally and internationally. Each organization has distinct goals and challenges. Therefore, we offer a wide range of data and AI services, carefully designed to cater to your requirements:

Data Strategy & Management

Creating a successful data strategy is fundamental for driving business growth. Our expert team defines a data strategy aligned with your organization's goals and we further guide you to translate it into actionable plans that seamlessly integrate with your people, processes, and technology.

Predicting What's Next

Stay ahead of the competition with predictive analytics powered by cutting-edge data science and machine learning techniques. Our solutions enable you to understand your customers on a deeper level, forecast future trends, and market dynamics, empowering you to make informed decisions with confidence.

Generative AI

Harnessing the potential of AI's generative capabilities can unlock novel opportunities for your organization. We specialize in identifying and implementing impactful use cases for foundational or open-source large language models, tailoring them to your specific industry and business needs.



Personalization

In the age of hyper-personalization, we enhance application data personalization using data science and machine learning. By applying classification and clustering techniques, we deliver custom recommendations, boosting customer loyalty and satisfaction. 

AI Driven Process Automation

Optimize your organization's processes and workflows through our expertise in process mining and robotic process automation. We ensure your operations are less error-prone, more efficient, cost-effective, and resilient, empowering your team to focus on high-value tasks.

Enhanced Insights

Data-driven insights are invaluable for strategic decision-making. Our experts are skilled in deploying descriptive analytics and creating business intelligence solutions that translate complex data into visually engaging management information dashboards.


The Best-Fit Products for Your Success

We believe in commoditizing wherever possible while being bespoke when needed, ensuring that our solutions are tailored to suit your unique requirements. We advocate for cloud-native practices and remain technology stack agnostic, selecting the best-fit products to achieve your objectives. Estedic is a registered consulting and system integration partner for both Databricks and Snowflake.
