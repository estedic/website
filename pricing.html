What is Data Management as a Service? 

Data management should not be a luxury reserved for the few. With Estedic's Data Management as a Service (DMaaS), businesses of all sizes can unlock the value of their data, guided by expert advice and supported by proven strategies. All this for a fixed monthly or yearly subscription fee. Discover how our subscription tiers can transform your data into a strategic asset, no matter your starting point.

Why Partner with Estedic for DMaaS?

  • Fixed costs: Expenses are known upfront to provide clarity and peace of mind.
  • Expertise on Demand: Gain access to top data management professionals without the need for a full-time hire.
  • Customized Solutions: Our tiered services are designed to meet you where you are, providing the right level of support for your business.
  • Scalability: As your business grows, our services scale with you, ensuring your data management capabilities keep pace.
  • Technology Agnostic: Benefit from unbiased advice and support across a wide range of technologies, ensuring the best fit for your needs.
